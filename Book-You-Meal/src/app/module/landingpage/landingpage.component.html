About Us

Browse

Book Your Meal has hundreds of restaurants to choose from. When you open the app, you can scroll through the feed for inspiration or search for a particular restaurant or cuisine. When you find something you like, tap to add it to your basket.

Order

When you’re ready to check out, you’ll see your address, an estimated delivery time and the price of the order including tax and delivery fee. When everything looks right, just tap Place order – and that’s it. We’ll automatically use the card on file, so you never need cash.

Our Values

Resilience

We push ourselves beyond our abilities when faced with tough times. When we foresee uncertainty, we address it only with flexibility.

Humility

It’s always ‘us’ over ‘me’. We don’t lose ourselves in pride or confidence during individual successes, but focus on being our simple selves in every which way.

Ownership

People here don’t work ‘for’ Book-Your-Meal, they work ‘with’ Book-Your-Meal. We treat every problem as our own, take accountability and drive the change.

Judgement

It’s not our abilities that show who we truly are - it’s our choices. We aim to get these right, at least in the majority of the cases.
